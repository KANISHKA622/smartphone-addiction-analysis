Screen time & sleep
Users with >8h daily screen time average only 5.4h sleep — 27% below the 7.4h baseline of non-addicted users.
Notifications & addiction
Severely addicted users receive 120+ notifications/day on average — 38% more than mild-addiction users (87/day).
Social media influence
Social media usage is the strongest single predictor of addiction (91% correlation). Severe users average 4.8h/day on social platforms.
Stress escalation
High-stress users average 9.1h screen time vs 5.8h for low-stress — a 57% gap suggesting bidirectional stress-usage feedback.
